Defense Fund Investment

We serve as the investment advisor to Athena Allied Defense Fund, a Luxembourg-based defense and security fund which brings together U.S. and European investors to support startups and small to medium size enterprises within NATO countries in Europe.

The founding partners of this investment advisor bring decades of experience in the public and private sector in the defense, security, and foreign policy spheres. Our expertise in defense policy, procurement mechanisms, evolving warfighter requirements, defense industry funding and investment, as well as our extensive network of defense industry leadership, startup ecosystem, and defense policy officials in the United States and in Europe offer invaluable insights into the future of European defense requirements and spending.

Advisory Services

We advise established and mature U.S. and European defense and security companies looking for growth and expansion opportunities in NATO countries.
